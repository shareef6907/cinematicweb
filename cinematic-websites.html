Why Video Headers Work

In a world of static websites, video immediately captures attention. Studies show that landing pages with video can increase conversions by up to 80%. But more importantly, video conveys what words and photos cannot:

  • Atmosphere: The feeling of your space, the energy of your brand
  • Movement: Dynamic content that holds attention
  • Emotion: Immediate emotional connection with visitors
  • Differentiation: Instant separation from template-based competitors

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the Cinematic Website package?
The package includes: pre-production planning, professional video shoot (15-30 second loop), post-production and web optimization, custom-coded responsive website, SEO foundation, Google Analytics setup, and 3 months of support.
How long does a Cinematic Website project take?
Typically 6-8 weeks from kickoff to launch. This includes 1-2 weeks pre-production, 1 week for video shoot and editing, and 4-5 weeks for web design and development.
Can you shoot video outside of Bahrain?
Yes, we can arrange video production throughout the GCC including Dubai, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, and Oman. Travel and accommodation are quoted separately.
Why is video on a homepage effective?
Video immediately captures attention, conveys brand personality, and creates emotional connection. Studies show video on landing pages can increase conversions by up to 80%. It also differentiates you from competitors using static images.
Will the video slow down my website?
No. We optimize all videos specifically for web delivery — compressed for fast loading while maintaining visual quality. We use modern lazy-loading techniques and fallback images to ensure fast initial page loads.
