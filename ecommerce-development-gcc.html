Frequently Asked Questions

Which ecommerce platform is best for GCC businesses?
It depends on your needs. Shopify is great for quick launch and ease of use. WooCommerce offers flexibility with WordPress. For high-volume stores, custom solutions with Next.js provide best performance. We recommend Shopify for most GCC businesses due to built-in payment gateways and Arabic support.
How much does an ecommerce website cost in Bahrain?
Ecommerce websites in Bahrain range from BD 1,500 for basic Shopify stores to BD 15,000+ for custom enterprise solutions. Most successful online stores invest BD 3,000-6,000 for a professional setup with payment integration and SEO.
What payment gateways work in the GCC?
Popular GCC payment gateways include Benefit (Bahrain), Tap Payments, PayTabs, Checkout.com, and Telr. We also integrate credit cards via Stripe for international transactions. Cash on delivery remains important in the region.
Can you build a bilingual Arabic/English store?
Yes, we build fully bilingual ecommerce stores with RTL Arabic support. Products, categories, checkout, and all content can be displayed in both languages with proper right-to-left formatting.
Do you provide ongoing ecommerce support?
Yes, we offer monthly maintenance plans including product updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and technical support. Plans start at BD 150/month depending on store complexity.
