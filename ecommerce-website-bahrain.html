Ecommerce That Converts

Launching an online store in Bahrain? You could go with a generic Shopify theme like everyone else. Or you could build something that truly represents your brand — a custom ecommerce experience that stands out and converts visitors into customers.

At Cinematic Web Works, we build custom ecommerce websites from scratch. No templates, no bloated plugins, no monthly platform fees eating into your margins. Just a beautiful, fast, and functional online store tailored to your specific business.

What We Build

  • Custom Product Catalogs: Beautiful product displays with multiple images, variants, and descriptions
  • Shopping Cart & Checkout: Streamlined purchasing experience optimized for conversion
  • Payment Integration: Stripe, PayPal, and regional payment gateways
  • Inventory Management: Stock tracking, low-stock alerts, and variant management
  • Order Management: Admin dashboard for tracking and managing orders
  • Customer Accounts: Optional registration, order history, and saved addresses
  • Shipping Calculation: Real-time shipping rates or fixed delivery fees

Payment Gateways

We integrate with the payment providers that work for Bahrain businesses:

  • Stripe: Credit/debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • PayPal: International payments and buyer protection
  • Regional Providers: Benefit, Tap, and local bank gateways
  • Cash on Delivery: Popular option for Bahrain customers

Why Custom Beats Templates

Template-based platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce have their place. But for serious brands, custom development offers clear advantages:

  • No Monthly Fees: You own your store outright — no subscription bleeding your profits
  • No Transaction Fees: Just the payment processor fee, not platform taxes
  • Unique Design: Your store looks nothing like your competitors
  • Performance: Custom code runs faster than bloated theme systems
  • Flexibility: Any feature you need can be built — no plugin hunting
  • SEO Control: Complete control over technical SEO implementation

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment gateways do you integrate?
We integrate Stripe, PayPal, and regional payment solutions. We can also integrate with Bahrain-specific payment providers and bank payment gateways as needed.
Do you use Shopify or WooCommerce?
We can work with these platforms if required, but we specialize in custom-coded ecommerce solutions that offer more flexibility, better performance, and no ongoing platform fees.
Can you handle inventory management?
Yes, we build custom inventory management systems tailored to your business needs — from simple stock tracking to complex multi-warehouse setups with variant management.
How long does it take to build an ecommerce website?
Typically 6-10 weeks depending on complexity. Simple stores with under 50 products take less time; complex multi-category stores with custom features take longer.
