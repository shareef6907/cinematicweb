Frequently Asked Questions

What features should a healthcare website have?

Online appointment booking, doctor profiles with credentials, service descriptions, patient testimonials (with consent), contact information with maps, insurance list, patient forms, and ideally a patient portal for secure communication.

How much does a healthcare website cost in Bahrain?

Healthcare website design ranges from BD 1,500 for a professional clinic website to BD 8,000+ for a full hospital website with patient portal and telemedicine features. Most private clinics invest BD 2,000-4,000.

Can you integrate appointment booking systems?

Yes, we integrate with healthcare booking systems or build custom solutions with calendar-based booking, doctor availability, automated reminders, and practice management integration.

Do you build patient portals?

Yes, we build secure patient portals where patients can view appointments, access test results, communicate with doctors, download invoices, and manage their health records.

Is my healthcare website secure?

Absolutely. We implement SSL encryption, secure hosting, and follow international healthcare data protection best practices to protect patient data.
