Frequently Asked Questions

What makes a great hotel website?

A great hotel website combines stunning visuals (ideally cinematic video), seamless booking integration, mobile-first design, fast load times, and compelling storytelling that captures the unique experience of staying at your property. It should make visitors feel like they're already there.

How much does a hotel website cost in Bahrain?

Hotel website design in Bahrain typically ranges from BD 2,000 to BD 10,000+ depending on features. A basic 5-page site starts around BD 2,000, while a full-featured site with booking integration, virtual tours, and cinematic video production ranges from BD 5,000 to BD 10,000+.

Do you integrate with hotel booking systems?

Yes, we integrate with all major hotel booking systems including Booking.com, Expedia, SynXis, Cloudbeds, Little Hotelier, and direct booking engines. We can also build custom booking solutions if you need something specific.

Can you shoot the video content for my hotel website?

Absolutely. Our founder is a professional filmmaker, so we offer complete video production services for hotel websites. We can shoot cinematic header videos, room tours, amenity showcases, and promotional content ‚Äî all optimized for web performance.

How long does it take to build a hotel website?

A typical hotel website takes 6-10 weeks from concept to launch. This includes discovery, design, development, content creation, booking integration, and testing. Video production may add 1-2 weeks depending on scope.
