Frequently Asked Questions

What is a landing page?
A landing page is a standalone web page designed for a specific campaign or offer. Unlike regular website pages, landing pages have one goal: convert visitors into leads or customers. They're used for ad campaigns, product launches, webinars, and lead magnets.
How much does a landing page cost?
Landing pages typically cost BD 400-1,500 depending on complexity. Simple lead capture pages start at BD 400. Complex pages with animations, video, and multiple sections range BD 800-1,500. We offer packages for multiple landing pages at discounted rates.
What makes a landing page convert?
High-converting landing pages have a clear headline, compelling offer, social proof (testimonials/logos), minimal distractions, strong call-to-action, fast loading speed, and mobile optimization. We design based on proven conversion principles.
How fast can you build a landing page?
We can deliver a landing page in 3-5 business days for standard projects. Rush delivery in 24-48 hours is available for urgent campaigns at an additional fee. Speed doesn't compromise quality â€” we have proven templates we customize.
Do you offer A/B testing?
Yes, we can set up A/B testing with multiple variations to optimize conversion rates. We recommend testing headlines, CTAs, and layouts. We'll provide data-driven recommendations based on test results.
