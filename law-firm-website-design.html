Frequently Asked Questions

What should a law firm website include?

Practice area pages, attorney profiles with credentials, case results or testimonials, contact form, firm history, location map, thought leadership blog, and ideally a client portal for secure document exchange.

How much does a law firm website cost?

Law firm websites range from BD 1,500 for a professional 8-10 page site to BD 5,000+ for larger firms with client portals. Most mid-sized firms invest BD 2,500-4,000.

Do you build client portals for law firms?

Yes, we build secure client portals where clients can access case documents, invoices, communicate with their legal team, and track case progress. All encrypted and access-controlled.

How important is SEO for law firms?

Very important. Many people search for lawyers online ('divorce lawyer Bahrain', 'corporate attorney Dubai'). A well-optimized website attracts high-value clients actively seeking legal services.

Can you help with legal content writing?

We coordinate with legal content specialists for practice area descriptions, attorney bios, and blog content. We also provide SEO guidance for legal content.
