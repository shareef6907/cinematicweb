Restaurant Websites That Make You Hungry

Your restaurant is about more than food — it's about atmosphere, experience, and emotion. Your website should convey the same feelings customers get when they walk through your door. At Cinematic Web Works, we specialize in creating restaurant websites that capture the essence of your venue.

Our founder's background in filmmaking brings a unique perspective to restaurant websites. We understand lighting, ambiance, and visual storytelling. The result? Websites that make visitors hungry and drive reservations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you integrate online menu systems?
Absolutely. We build custom menu systems that you can update easily — including prices, items, photos, and specials. Menus can be categorized by course, diet type, or any system that works for you.
Do you integrate reservation systems?
Yes, we can integrate reservation systems like OpenTable, Resy, or build custom booking functionality. We also integrate with WhatsApp for direct booking inquiries.
Can you add video to show the restaurant atmosphere?
This is our specialty! Our founder is a professional filmmaker. We offer Cinematic Website packages that include professionally-shot video headers showcasing your restaurant's atmosphere.
How do you make restaurant websites rank on Google?
We implement Restaurant schema markup, local SEO best practices, and ensure your Google Business Profile is properly linked. We optimize for searches like 'restaurants near me' and specific cuisine types.
