Frequently Asked Questions

What features should a salon website have?

Online booking, service menu with prices, staff profiles, before/after gallery, client reviews, Instagram integration, and gift card purchasing. Mobile-first design is essential.

How much does a salon website cost in Bahrain?

From BD 600 for a simple site to BD 2,500+ for a full-featured site with e-commerce and custom booking. Most salons invest BD 1,000-1,500.

Can you integrate online booking?

Yes, we integrate with Fresha, Booksy, Square Appointments, and Vagaro. We can also build custom booking solutions with staff selection and reminders.

Do you provide photography for salon websites?

Yes, our founder is a professional photographer. We can shoot your salon interior, staff portraits, and before/after transformations.

Can I sell products online?

Absolutely. We add e-commerce for retail products, gift cards, and service packages with secure checkout and delivery options.
