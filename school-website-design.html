Frequently Asked Questions

What features should a school website have?

Online admissions portal, parent/student login areas, academic calendar, news and announcements, faculty profiles, curriculum information, photo galleries, contact forms, and ideally SIS and LMS integration.

How much does a school website cost in Bahrain?

School website design ranges from BD 2,000 for a professional informational site to BD 8,000+ for a full-featured site with parent portal, admissions system, and LMS integration. Most schools invest BD 3,000-5,000.

Can you integrate with existing school management systems?

Yes, we integrate with PowerSchool, Blackbaud, ManageBac, and others. We can also build custom integrations with your existing SIS.

Do you build parent portals?

Yes, we build secure parent portals with grades, attendance, teacher communication, fee payment, and announcements ‚Äî all with role-based access control.

Can you help with online admissions?

Absolutely. We build complete online admissions systems with application forms, document uploads, tracking, payment, and admin dashboards.
