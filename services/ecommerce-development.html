Our E-Commerce Development Process

Building a successful online store requires careful planning and execution. Our process ensures nothing is overlooked, from product architecture to checkout optimization.

Strategy Phase: Before writing any code, we map out your entire e-commerce operation. How are products organized? What payment methods do your customers prefer? How will shipping work? What about returns? This phase ensures we build a store that fits your business, not the other way around.

Design Phase: E-commerce design is a specialized discipline. We design product pages that showcase your items compellingly, category pages that help customers browse efficiently, and checkout flows that minimize friction and cart abandonment. Every element is tested against conversion best practices.

Build Phase: Using Next.js and headless commerce backends, we build your store with performance and scalability in mind. Product catalogs, shopping carts, inventory management, payment processing, order fulfillment — all integrated seamlessly into a fast, beautiful frontend.

Launch Phase: We don't just hand over the keys and walk away. Launch includes comprehensive testing, staff training, documentation, and ongoing support to ensure your store runs smoothly from day one.

Technology Stack

We use a modern headless commerce architecture that separates your storefront from your backend, allowing for maximum performance and flexibility:

  • Next.js 14: Ultra-fast React framework for the storefront with server-side rendering for SEO
  • Headless Commerce Backends: Shopify Hydrogen, Medusa, Saleor, or custom Supabase solutions depending on your needs
  • Tailwind CSS: Custom-designed interfaces without template limitations
  • Vercel Edge Network: Global CDN deployment for sub-second page loads worldwide

This architecture means your store loads 3-5x faster than traditional platforms like WooCommerce or Magento, directly impacting your conversion rates and search rankings.

Frequently Asked Questions

What payment gateways do you integrate with?
We integrate with all major payment gateways available in the GCC including Benefit Pay, CrediMax, Tap Payments, Stripe, PayTabs, and more. We can also integrate with regional solutions like STC Pay and Apple Pay for seamless checkout experiences.
How much does an e-commerce website cost?
E-commerce projects start at BD 2,000 for a standard online store with up to 100 products. Complex stores with advanced features like multi-vendor marketplaces, subscriptions, or custom configurators range from BD 4,000 to BD 10,000+. We provide detailed quotes after understanding your specific requirements.
Do you build on Shopify or WooCommerce?
We specialize in custom headless commerce solutions using Next.js with backends like Shopify Hydrogen, Medusa, or Saleor. This gives you the flexibility of custom design with the reliability of proven e-commerce backends. We can also work with existing Shopify/WooCommerce stores if needed.
How do you handle inventory management?
Our e-commerce solutions include robust inventory management with real-time stock tracking, low-stock alerts, and optional integration with your existing inventory systems or ERPs. For complex needs, we can integrate with solutions like Zoho Inventory or SAP.
Can you migrate my existing online store?
Yes, we handle full migrations from platforms like WooCommerce, Shopify, Magento, and others. We transfer all products, customers, order history, and SEO data to ensure a seamless transition with minimal disruption to your business.
Do you provide training on managing the store?
Absolutely. Every e-commerce project includes comprehensive training for your team covering product management, order processing, inventory updates, and reporting. We also provide documentation and video tutorials for future reference.
