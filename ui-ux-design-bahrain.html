What is UI/UX Design?

UX (User Experience) is the overall feel and usability of your product. It answers: Is it easy to use? Can users find what they need? Do they enjoy the experience?

UI (User Interface) is the visual layer — the buttons, colors, typography, and layouts that users interact with. Good UI makes the UX tangible and beautiful.

We specialize in both, creating products that work intuitively AND look stunning.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is UI/UX design?
UI (User Interface) design focuses on the visual elements users interact with — buttons, icons, typography, colors, and layouts. UX (User Experience) design focuses on the overall feel and usability — how easy and pleasant it is to use your website or app. Good UI/UX design combines both to create products that look great AND work intuitively.
How much does UI/UX design cost in Bahrain?
UI/UX design in Bahrain typically costs BD 500-3,000+ depending on project scope. A website redesign with wireframes and mockups starts around BD 500-1,000. A complete UX overhaul with user research, multiple iterations, and design system creation ranges BD 1,500-3,000+. Mobile app design projects typically fall in the BD 800-2,000 range.
Do you offer design-only services without development?
Yes, we offer standalone UI/UX design services. We can deliver Figma files, design systems, and specifications that your development team can implement. However, our best results come from projects where we handle both design and development — ensuring the final product matches the design perfectly.
How long does a UI/UX design project take?
A typical website UI/UX project takes 2-4 weeks. This includes discovery, wireframing, visual design, and revisions. Larger projects with extensive user research or multiple products can take 4-8 weeks. We always provide a timeline estimate before starting.
What deliverables do I receive?
Deliverables include: wireframes (low-fidelity layouts), high-fidelity mockups for all pages/screens, interactive prototypes, style guide with colors/fonts/components, and Figma source files. For comprehensive projects, we also provide user research reports and design system documentation.
