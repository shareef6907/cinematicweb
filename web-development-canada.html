Do you support bilingual English/French websites?

Absolutely. We build fully bilingual websites with proper internationalization architecture, ensuring SEO optimization for both English and French Canadian markets. Our technical approach uses Next.js i18n routing for clean URLs like yoursite.com/fr/ and proper hreflang tags for search engines.

How do you handle Canadian accessibility requirements?

AODA compliance is built into our development process. We follow WCAG 2.1 AA standards, implement proper semantic HTML, ensure keyboard navigation, and test with screen readers. This isn't an afterthought—it's part of how we build every site.

What about payment processing and e-commerce?

We integrate with Stripe (fully Canadian-compliant), Shopify Payments, and other Canadian-friendly payment processors. GST/HST calculation, Canadian shipping carriers, and multi-currency support are all part of our e-commerce expertise.
