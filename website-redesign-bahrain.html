Redesign vs. New Build

Sometimes a redesign isn't enough. If your current platform is fundamentally broken (slow, insecure, impossible to update), we might recommend starting fresh. We'll be honest about what makes sense for your situation and budget.

That said, a redesign can breathe new life into your online presence without starting from scratch. We preserve what works ‚Äî your domain authority, your content, your brand recognition ‚Äî while upgrading the rest.

Frequently Asked Questions

When should I redesign my website?
Consider a redesign if your website is over 3 years old, not mobile-responsive, slow to load, has high bounce rates, doesn't reflect your current brand, or isn't converting visitors. If customers complain or you're embarrassed to share your URL, it's time.
How much does a website redesign cost in Bahrain?
Website redesigns in Bahrain typically range from BD 1,500 for simple refresh to BD 8,000+ for complete overhauls with new functionality. Most business website redesigns fall in the BD 2,500-4,500 range.
Will I lose my Google rankings during redesign?
Not if done properly. We implement 301 redirects, preserve URL structures where possible, maintain meta tags, and submit updated sitemaps. Our redesigns typically improve rankings due to better performance and mobile experience.
How long does a website redesign take?
A typical website redesign takes 4-8 weeks depending on complexity. This includes discovery, design, development, content migration, testing, and launch. We can expedite for urgent projects.
Can you keep my current content during redesign?
Yes, we can migrate all existing content to your new design. We also offer content refresh services if your text needs updating. Images may need optimization for the new design.
